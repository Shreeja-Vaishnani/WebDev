About Charusat

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is a leading private university located in Gujarat, India.

Founded in 2000, CHARUSAT offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, pharmacy, computer applications, and more.

The university is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation, providing students with state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant campus life.

CHARUSAT aims to create future leaders and professionals who contribute to society through knowledge, skills, and ethics.
