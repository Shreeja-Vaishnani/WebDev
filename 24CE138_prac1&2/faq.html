FAQ

Q: How can I register on the student portal?

Click on "Register" in the navigation menu and fill out the form with your details.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password" to reset your credentials.

Q: Can I update my profile details?

A: Yes, after logging in, you can visit your Profile page and edit your information.
